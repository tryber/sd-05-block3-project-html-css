Top 5 Popular Breeds - MOST FAMOUS!


Well, each person has a preference for breeds depending on their sizes, or maybe how cute they are, or even if they can defend their owners! I mean, their beloved friends! What are the top 5 popular dogs?

  1. Cocker Spaniel

    2. Loyal and playful: the words that define the Cocker Spaniel. It is the smallest of the spaniels. The perfect choice for someone looking for a big fella. Despite the hunting instinct, this breed is very loving and dedicated.

  2. Springer Spaniel

    3. Like the Cocker, the Spring Spaniel is also very fun and affectionate. He is smart and obedient and is often used in a way to spend a lot of energy, like field hunting.

  3. Labrador Retriever

    4. The Labrador Retriever is so simpatic, it is impossible to not love it. Also very loyal, it can be used to support people with special necessities. If there is something that you can't say aboutthe Labrador Retriever is that they are shy or agressive.

  4. Cockapoo

    5. Cockapoos hate to be alone, you need to be always around. They will be asking for your attention all the time, following you wherever you go. If you are a home person, he is certainly the ideal mate.

  5. Staffordshire Bull Terrier

    6. If you look at Staffordshire Bull Terrier's face, you'll probably think: "this guy is angry!". That is not the truth though, they actually have a big heart. It is very lovely and will be always on your side.

Top 5 Dangerous Breeds - BE CAREFUL!


Yes, dogs are real friends, but they also have self-defense instinct.Given this fact, some of them can be agressive and dangerous in some situations. Find below the top 5 breeds with higher tendency of presenting this behavior.

  1. Perro de Presa Canario

    2. Initially bred to protect livestock, the Perro de Presa Canario is dominant and needs to be socialized early. If not well trained, they can be very agressive to other dogs and strangers.

  2. Caucasian Shepherd

    3. Also bred to protect livestock, the strong and fearless Caucasian Sheperd, also known as Caucasian Ovcharka, this dog is sometimes even feared by wolves.

  3. German Shepherd

    4. German Sheperds are very intelligent and are commonly used in police departments. But if put in a bad situation, they can become very agressive with a strong bite and brute force.

  4. Rottweiler

    5. Rottweilers are extremely loyal, confident and courageous. They do everything to protect their family and that is the reason they are commonly used as a guard dog. They appear to be very calm, just don't try to mess up with them.

  5. Pitbull Terrier

    6. Strong and agile, Pitbulls are known for being dangerous to families, specially with kids. This breed has a high number of attacks against humans.
