Main Fan Outrage! Why it doesn't matter

The Last of Us Part 2 leaked by hackers, reports say

An exploit in past Naughty Dog games gave hackers access to TLOU2 files

Alexander Mitchell | @AlexanderMitchell

Unidentified hackers appear to have been responsible for the massive
leak of The Last of Us Part 2 months ahead of the game’s release,
according to multiple reports. Those hackers reportedly gained access to
files relating to The Last of Us Part 2 by exploiting a vulnerability in
previous games developed by Naughty Dog.

In late April, key, spoiler-filled plot points and level details from
The Last of Us Part 2 were posted to YouTube, revealing much of the
story from Naughty Dog’s upcoming PlayStation 4 game. A statement from
Sony Interactive Entertainment said that the persons responsible for
that leak were not affiliated with Naughty Dog or SIE.

Plot details lead to fan outrage

  • Shocking spoiler details

    • Plot leaks reveal that you only play halfway through the game as the
    beloved characters from the original, a fact that always upsets gamers
    since Metal Gear Solid 2

  • Alleged change in tone of the original series

    • Game enthusiasts have also been commenting on a apparent change in the
    core atmosphere of the game, due to new characters, and a change of
    focus from the original main characters.

Why none of that matters?

  1. The game hasn't come out yet

    2. theres no way to confirm if these story leaks actually have a negative
    impact on the game. Given Naughty Dog’s track record there must be a
    great reason for all plot elements involved.

  2. Naughty Dog is trying to do something different

    3. By focusing more on female protagonists, the company is trying to fill
    a gap that currently exists, and a lack of female role models in the
    medium for a traditionally "gatekeeped industry"

  3. Most of the outrage comes from an already established trait of the
    protagonist.

    4. A big deal of what alleged fans are outraged revolves around the
    bigger focus of Ellie and her sexuality. A trait previously
    established in the original game and "DLC".
